# Trader Agreement Rules (must be accepted before matching)

By joining the SiteVerdict Trusted Trader Network, a trader agrees to:
- No misleading claims about work, qualifications, or SiteVerdict.
- No pressure selling. Give the client space to decide.
- Protect client privacy — never share or on-sell client details.
- Show up when booked; if unable, communicate early.
- Communicate clearly and promptly.
- Quote fairly — no bait pricing, no surprise add-ons.
- Do not misrepresent SiteVerdict or imply SiteVerdict guarantees the work.
- Do not contact a client until T has approved the match and the client has consented.
- Understand that bad behaviour can lead to suspension or ban.
- Understand that client feedback affects ranking and future matches.
- Understand that T / SiteVerdict can remove a trader from the network at any time.

These are conduct rules, not a guarantee of work. SiteVerdict offers client-first matching;
it does not promise a guaranteed job, guaranteed result, or any specific volume of leads.
