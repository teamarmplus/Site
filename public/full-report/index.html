What the report includes
The Full Report builds on the free Site Check and goes further. Depending on the site and purpose, it may include title and DP review, deeper planning controls, constraint analysis, civil and service risk assessment, cost and feasibility context, and professional verification referrals. Not all elements are automated — some require licensed professional input. Confirm scope and timing before ordering.
Zoning and planning controls from the NSW LEP
Lot size and development pathway estimate
Overlays and constraints — heritage, flood, bushfire, acid sulfate, contaminated land, and riparian / watercourse indicators where available or flagged for professional verification
DA timeline context — council median approval time for subdivision DAs
Council behaviour summary — how active the council is on residential DAs
Risk register — what needs verification, what is clear, what is uncertain
Professional verification checklist — what a planner, surveyor or engineer would look at next
Service and finance next-step options based on what the report finds
