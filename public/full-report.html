What happens in a review

1

Start with your free Site Check

Run a free Site Check on your property address. We show you what we found from official data sources — zone, land size, council, overlays, and what is still missing.

2

Tell us what you want to understand

Submit a simple enquiry — sell, lease, develop, improve, refinance, or simply understand what the land may be able to do. You can attach photos, plans, or council letters if you have them.

3

We review what the Site Check found

Our team reviews your Site Check result, the data that was confirmed, and what is still missing. We consider what the land may be able to do after the missing checks are completed.

4

We give you a clear picture of the next step

We tell you what extra information may be needed, what professionals may need to check the land, what pathways may be worth reviewing, and what the safest first step may be.

5

Professional Pathway where needed

If a planner, surveyor, civil engineer, or other professional is needed, we help connect you through the Professional Pathway. Matched support is available where suitable providers are available.

What may be possible

Sell or prepare for sale

Understanding zone, overlays, and land size before listing can help you understand what a buyer may see — and what a professional marketing plan may look like.

Lease or prepare for lease

Site context and council information can help you and a leasing professional understand what uses may be appropriate and what the land may support.

Subdivide or develop

If the zone and land size suggest a land potential review may be worth doing, a professional assessment of feasibility, cost, approvals, and risk is the right next step.

Improve land value

Sometimes a relatively small investment in civil works, access, drainage, or landscaping can materially affect what the land is worth and how quickly it sells or leases.

Refinance or finance preparation

Planning-risk context can help conversations with a licensed broker or lender. A lender-ready planning summary may support your finance application or review.

Not sure yet

That is fine. Many people are not sure what they want to do with their land. A review can help you understand what the land may be able to do so you can make a calmer, better-informed decision.
