# Sydney Home Improve Outreach Scripts

Use respectful one-to-one outreach only. Do not mass-email or spam. If a referral fee or arrangement applies, disclose it clearly to the client and the referrer.

## Builder referral message

Hi, I’m T from Sydney Home Improve.

We help builders and owners with external works near completion — driveways, concrete, drainage, retaining walls, turf, landscaping, excavation and small civil works.

If any of your clients need help getting outside works finished for handover or OC, I’m happy to quote quickly and look after them properly.

If any referral arrangement applies, we keep it transparent and disclose it clearly.

Would you be open to me sending a short services list?

## Homeowner message

Hi, I’m T from Sydney Home Improve.

We’re taking on a few local jobs for concrete, driveways, retaining walls, drainage, landscaping, excavation and small civil works.

If you need a quote, send the address, photos and what needs doing. Free to enquire, no obligation.

## DA near-completion message

Hi, I’m T from Sydney Home Improve.

We help with practical external works that often come near the end of a build or DA project: driveway, concrete, retaining walls, drainage, turf, landscaping and site clean-up.

If the project needs help before handover or OC, send the address, photos/plans and timing. I’m happy to review and quote.

## Follow-up message

Hi, just checking whether you’d like me to quote the external works this week. You can send photos, address and rough timing, and I’ll let you know the next step. No obligation.

## Intake checklist

- Name
- Phone
- Property address
- Service needed
- Photos/plans available
- Timing
- OC/handover deadline if relevant
- Owner / builder / developer / agent
